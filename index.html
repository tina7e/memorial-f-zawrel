Welcome

This is a little page in memory of Friedrich Zawrel, Viennese survivor of the Nazi child euthanasia program. You will learn about key moments in Friedrich Zawrel's biography with links to more detailed background information. Zawrel's life story serves not only as witness report to the gruesome cruelty of medicine and psychiatry under the ideology of "racial hygiene" but also to the continuity of lucrative careers begun under that ideology that were allowed to prosper until long after 1945.
