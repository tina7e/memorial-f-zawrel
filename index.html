Introduction

This memorial page features key moments in Friedrich Zawrel's biography.
Zawrel's life story serves not only as witness report to the gruesome cruelty of medicine and psychiatry under the ideology of "racial hygiene" but also to the continuity of lucrative careers begun under that ideology that were allowed to prosper until long after 1945.
The main text is kept brief and concise but you are welcome to follow the pop-up links to additional information as you scroll along. You may also want to peruse the link collection under the "Resources" tab.
